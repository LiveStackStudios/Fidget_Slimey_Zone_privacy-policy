Privacy Policy — Fidget Slimey Zone

Last updated: April 29, 2026 · Effective date: April 29, 2026

Fidget Slimey Zone ("the App") is operated by Livestack Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and, depending on your settings, your name and email address. This is used solely to manage your account, sync your progress, and process purchases. You may use the app without signing in — anonymous access is supported.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, session length, and in-app activity. This helps us improve the experience.

Purchase Information: If you make an in-app purchase, transaction data is processed by Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card information.

Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect your device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) and usage data to show relevant ads. On iOS, we will ask for your permission before accessing your IDFA via the App Tracking Transparency prompt. You may decline and ads will still be shown, but they will be less personalized.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: operate and improve the App; process and restore purchases; show advertisements (free tier users); track your progress, coins, XP, and unlocks; and respond to support requests.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Firebase — analytics and anonymous authentication
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management
  • Sign in with Apple — optional authentication
  • Sign in with Google — optional authentication

4. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for all ages including families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to operate the app. We do not share children's data with third parties for advertising purposes. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information without parental consent, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Because the App is rated for all ages and uses advertising, AdMob is configured to serve family-safe ads only. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google's Families Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Anonymous accounts with no purchases are deleted after 12 months of inactivity.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. EU and California residents have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. For EU users, a consent form will be presented on first launch in accordance with GDPR requirements.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your device secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
