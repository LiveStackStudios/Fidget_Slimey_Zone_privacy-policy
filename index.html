1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Fidget Slimey Zone ("the App", "we", "us", "our") collects, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have over it. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

LiveStack Studios operates the App and is the data controller for any personal data processed under this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Only on Your Device

The following data is stored locally in your device's storage and is never transmitted to any server. It is permanently erased when you delete the app or use the in-app reset function.

  • In-game progress: coin balance, gem balance, XP, level, daily streak, total pops, total clicks
  • Slime collection: saved slime recipes including color, texture, decoration choices, container type, and custom name
  • Unlocks: which toys, pop-its, colors, and decorations you have unlocked
  • Settings preferences: sound, haptics, theme color
  • Subscription tier cached locally for offline use
  • Daily session counters: coins earned today, rewarded ads watched today

2.2 Anonymous User Identifier

On first launch, the App generates an anonymous user ID. This ID is used to create an anonymous credential with Firebase Authentication and is sent to RevenueCat as an app user ID for entitlement and subscription management. It does not identify you personally in any way.

2.3 Account Data (Only If You Choose to Sign In)

Sign-in is entirely optional. You can use all core features anonymously. If you choose to sign in:

Apple Sign In — we receive an Apple relay token and, if you grant permission, your name and email address.

Google Sign In — we receive a Google ID token, your email address, display name, and profile photo URL.

This data is stored in Firebase Firestore under your user ID to enable cross-device progress sync. Signing out removes your local credential but does not automatically delete your Firestore record. To request deletion of your server-side data, contact us at ja@livestackstudios.com.

2.4 Crash and Diagnostic Data

Firebase Crashlytics — automatically collected when the app crashes. Data includes stack trace, OS version, device model, app version, and an anonymous Firebase installation ID. No personally identifiable information is included.

Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous event data such as level_up, slime_saved, and purchase_complete. This data is tied to a Firebase installation ID, not to your personal identity.

2.5 Advertising Data

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. Ads appear on the Home, Toys, and Slime Play screens (banner), every fifth toy navigation (interstitial), and as an optional rewarded ad capped at 3 per day in exchange for in-game coins.

On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before any ad is loaded. If you grant permission, AdMob may receive your Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) to show personalised ads. If you deny permission, only non-personalised contextual ads are shown and your IDFA is never accessed.

For users in the European Union, a Google UMP consent dialog is displayed on first launch. Your consent choice is stored and respected on every subsequent launch.

All ad inventory is configured to family-safe content with a maximum content rating of Parental Guidance.

2.6 Purchase Data

All payment transactions are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We never receive or store your credit card or payment information. RevenueCat receives your anonymous app user ID, transaction receipts relayed from Apple, product IDs purchased, and your current entitlement state to manage your subscription and support purchase restoration across devices.

2.7 What We Do Not Collect

  • Precise or approximate location
  • Camera, microphone, or photo library data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Health or biometric data
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Any data from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to operate the App

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate and deliver the App's features and gameplay
  • Restore your purchases and subscription entitlements across devices
  • Show advertisements to free-tier users
  • Detect and fix crashes and performance issues
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the App
  • Enforce daily reward caps and prevent abuse of the in-game economy
  • Respond to support requests you send us

6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences including families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to operate the App, and we do not share any such information with third parties for advertising purposes.

All ad inventory is restricted to family-safe content. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us at ja@livestackstudios.com and we will promptly delete it.

  • Delete local data by resetting the app or deleting it from your device

  • Restore purchases via Settings → Restore Purchases within the App

EU Users (GDPR — Articles 15–20)

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

California Users (CCPA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise any of these rights, email ja@livestackstudios.com. We will respond within 30 days.
